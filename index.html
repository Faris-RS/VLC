VLC Media Player

TVLC is a free and open source cross-platform multimedia player and framework that plays most multimedia files as well as DVDs, Audio CDs, VCDs, and various streaming protocols.

Other projects from VideoLAN


VLC Media Player

VLC is a powerful media player playing most of the media codecs and video formats out there.

DV Blast

DV Blast is a simple and powerful MPEG-2/TS demux and streaming application

VLMC

VideoLAN Movie Creator is a non-linear editing software for video creation.

X264

x264 is a free application for encoding video streams into H.264/MPEG-4 AVC format.
